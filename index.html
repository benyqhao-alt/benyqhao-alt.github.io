Median Family Weekly Income

(每周家庭收入中位數)


Filter returns the median income across families residing across Melbourne. Suburbs with a high individual income but lower family income may suggest more independent residents.

(每周房子收入中位數)


Filter provides the median income earned households by suburbs. High income data for this filter may suggest boarding homes or flatsharing situations if not positively correlated with family incomes.
